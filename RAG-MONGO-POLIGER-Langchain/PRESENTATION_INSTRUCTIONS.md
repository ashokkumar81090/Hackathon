### Opening (30 seconds)
"Good morning/afternoon. I'm here to present a solution that can save our organization **$5.9 million annually** while improving our IT operations efficiency by 44%."

### Slide 1 Focus (2 minutes)
1. **Lead with the $5.9M ROI** - This gets immediate attention
2. **Break down the savings**:
   - $2.4M from faster resolution
   - $1.8M from automation
   - $1.2M from better first-call resolution
3. **Emphasize quick wins**: 44% faster, 38% automation

### Slide 2 Focus (2 minutes)
1. **Show performance metrics** - Concrete before/after numbers
2. **Highlight strategic value**:
   - Knowledge retention
   - Risk mitigation

3. **Address implementation**: Low risk, quick deployment, fast payback

### Closing (30 seconds)
"Based on these metrics, I recommend we proceed with a 2-week pilot program to validate these ROI calculations with our actual data. This will give us concrete numbers before full deployment."
